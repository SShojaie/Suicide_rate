Bootcamp group project: The Pursuit of Happiness

Project Specifics

Utilize Python, Pandas, Numpy and, Matplotlib and other visualization libraries to analyze and answer the following questions:
  1. How does the Happiness Index vary across the globe?
  2. Does economic health correlate with happiness?
  3. What other factors impact happiness?
  4. How do happy countries relative to their economic health rank on the Happiness Index variables?
  5. How do happy countries relative to their economic health rank on other factors correlated with happiness?
  6. How does Canada perform on the Happiness Index variables and how does it compare to the US?
  7. Is happiness changing over time?
